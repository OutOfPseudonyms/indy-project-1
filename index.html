About Me

I am from the dying town of Danville, Illinois. The first six years of my life were spent around the general area, then my dad decided he wanted to move back across the border to his hometown in Indiana. Around the time I was thirteen, he started dragging me down to northern Kentucky and back. What I was not fond of was when we moved to way-too-hot Florida. Fast forward to today, I am a programming student living in the Pacific Northwest. Gaming has been a main interest of mine since before I learned how much I love to read. I like to read mostly science fiction and various forms of fantasy. I have always had an interest in programming, so I decided to enter a coding boot camp. I've had a little experience debugging code and found that I enjoyed it, and I am interested in pursuing it as a career.
